About Us

We are awesome TEAM

The IETE core team comprises dedicated and passionate individuals who lead and drive the activities of the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) branch. They work collaboratively, organizing workshops, events, and competitions, and ensure the smooth functioning of the branch. With their expertise and commitment, the core team strives to create a vibrant and enriching environment for IETE members, promoting technical knowledge, professional development, and networking opportunities.


The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's leading recognised professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication & IT. Founded in 1953. The IETE is the National Apex Professional body of Electronics and Telecommunication, Computer Science and IT Professionals. It serves more than 1,25,000 members (including Corporate, Student and ISF members) through various 63 Centres, spread all over India and abroad. The Institution provides leadership in Scientific and Technical areas of direct importance to the national development and economy. Government of India has recognised IETE as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) and also notified as an educational Institution of national eminence. The objectives of IETE focus on advancing electro-technology. The IETE conducts and sponsors technical meetings, conferences, symposia, and exhibitions all over India, publishes technical journals and provides continuing education as well as career advancement opportunities to its members. The IETE focuses on advancement of the Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, Computers, Information Technology and related areas. Towards this end the Institution promotes and conducts basic engineering and continuing technical education programmes for human resource development.



What makes us different ?

The core team of a student chapter is a dedicated group of individuals who lead and manage the activities and initiatives of the chapter. Comprising students from various backgrounds and disciplines, the core team works collaboratively to achieve the chapter's objectives and promote its mission. Overall, the core team of a student chapter plays a vital role in shaping the chapter's activities and creating a positive impact on the student community. Through their leadership, coordination, and enthusiasm, they inspire and empower members to explore their interests, develop new skills, and contribute to the collective growth and success of the chapter.

Benefits of joining IETE

Leadership Opportunities

Student chapters offer numerous leadership roles and responsibilities to their members. Serving on the core team or taking up other leadership positions allows students to enhance their leadership abilities, decision-making skills, and organizational management skills. These experiences can be valuable in their personal and professional growth.

Industry Exposure

Many student chapters collaborate with industry partners, inviting professionals to deliver guest lectures, organize industry visits, and facilitate internships or job placements. This exposure to the industry gives members insights into real-world applications, current trends, and industry best practices, preparing them for their future careers.

Networking Opportunities

Student chapters offer excellent networking opportunities for members. Students can connect with like-minded individuals, industry professionals, and experts in their field of interest. This networking can lead to valuable connections, mentorship opportunities, internships, and future collaborations.

Skill Development

Student chapters provide a conducive environment for skill development. Through workshops, seminars, competitions, and hands-on projects, members can acquire new skills and enhance their existing ones. These practical learning experiences help students develop their technical, leadership, communication, and teamwork skills, making them more competent and marketable.

Recognition & Awards

Student chapters often participate in competitions and events organized by the parent organization or other institutions. Winning awards and accolades not only brings recognition to the chapter but also boosts the confidence and motivation of its members. It showcases their talents and achievements on a broader platform.
